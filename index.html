About

Ariel Birnbaum

Ariel Birnbaum was born in New York in 1987 and moved with his family to Israel at the age of 4. Ariel served in the IDF for 3 years as a combat medic and has been a professional tour guide since 2011. Today he is a student of History and Israel studies at the Open University.

As a licensed tour guide, he emphasizes the history of Israel and the significance of Jerusalem. He has guided groups from all backgrounds, including Christian pilgrim groups, youth groups, study tours and Jewish root tours. Whether it is your first visit in Israel, or your second or third, Ariel will make sure you get to see the land thrugh his eyes
